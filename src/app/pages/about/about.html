Our Mission

Bridging the Gap Between In-House and Outsourcing

Traditional outsourcing gives you a vendor with pooled resources and limited accountability. Building your own offshore team from scratch is expensive, slow, and risky.

OpenBaseAfrica fills the gap: we build a fully functioning operation, office, team, systems, and daily management, that runs like your own, at a fraction of the cost. You keep control. We handle the burden.

Our Vision

The Operating Partner of Choice for Global Companies Entering Africa

We see a future where any ambitious company, regardless of size or geography, can build a world-class African operation without navigating the complexity alone.

Our goal is to be the infrastructure layer that makes that possible: trusted, transparent, and relentlessly execution-focused.
