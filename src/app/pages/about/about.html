Our Story

The Full U.S. Ticket Brokerage Story

Base Africa started as a U.S. ticket brokerage initially serving the localized market. Over a decade, this company evolved and diversified into managing local and international ticket brokering, adapting to a dynamic market and expanding its reach to cater to a diverse array of global clients and demands.

In 2024, Base Africa expanded its operations to the continent of Africa, where it has grown into a powerful strategic partner for businesses. We provide end-to-end support for foreign companies eager to enter the vibrant African markets and establish a strong operational presence across the continent.
